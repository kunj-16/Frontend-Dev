AI & Automation: A Vision by Kunj Bhasin

Hello! I’m Kunj Bhasin, a B.Tech student at GLA University, Mathura. My focus lies in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Automation. I enjoy developing real-world projects that merge deep learning and computer vision with practical applications.

My academic journey and club activities through the DROID Automation Club have strengthened my skills in AI-driven innovation and technical leadership. I’ve worked on several impactful projects that aim to bridge human understanding with intelligent technology.

Some of my recent projects include a SAR Image Colorization model for better satellite data interpretation, a Real-Time Speed Monitoring System for road safety, and a Skin Disease Prediction app that assists in preliminary medical diagnosis using image processing.

In the ever-evolving world of AI and ML, I aspire to keep exploring new technologies that simplify human experiences and make automation smarter, safer, and more efficient.

Through consistent learning, collaboration, and experimentation, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the advancement of intelligent systems that help shape a more innovative and connected future.
