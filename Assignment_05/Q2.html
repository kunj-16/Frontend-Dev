Welcome to Banaras

Banaras, also known as Varanasi, is one of the oldest living cities in the world, situated on the banks of the sacred Ganga River in India.

Culture and Spirituality

Banaras is the spiritual capital of India, known for its temples, rituals, music, and art. It is a center of Hindu culture and ancient learning.

Banarasi Cuisine

Banaras is famous for its street food like kachori-sabzi, tamatar chaat, and sweets such as jalebi and the iconic Banarasi paan.
