About Microsoft

Microsoft is a global technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington. Founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975, Microsoft develops software, hardware, and cloud-based solutions that empower people and organizations to achieve more.

  • Microsoft Office 365 – Productivity Software

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Tools
